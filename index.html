ADHD treatment

We provide ADHD treatment for hyperactive children of age group 2 to 17 years old. Hyperactivity is a sign of Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD). ADHD in children can also happen with other conditions.

Natural treatment

We prefer natural treatment over pills. In yoga, depression is handled at the level of the body, mind and the energies. Is yoga effective for treating depression? Studies show that yoga therapy can help with stress, anxiety, and depression. Yoga is a gentle exercise that incorporates both meditation and controlled, physical movements.

Free from Depression

Our clinical approach is specifically tailored to address the life needs and struggles of those burdened with severe and chronic mental illness. The HealMe offers outpatient individual therapy, psychological testing as well as specialized interventions for those suffering from chronic mental illness including residential services.

Healing from Trauma

When bad things happen, it can take a while to get over the pain and feel safe again. But with these self-help strategies and support, you can speed up your recovery. Our whole team is whith you. Whatever the cause of your trauma, and whether it happened years ago or yesterday, you can make healing changes and move on with your life.
